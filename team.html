Our Mission

district0x aims to facilitate the operation and governing of marketplaces and communities as decentralized autonomous organizations. district0x seeks to enable the recreation of many of the internet’s most popular applications and services while eliminating rent-seeking and the relinquishment of decisions and personal data to third-parties.
